Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out policy?
Standard check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. We offer early arrival and late departure options for members, subject to availability. All access is handled via our secure digital key system.
How does the digital concierge service work?
Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive access to our mobile app. You can chat directly with your Lifestyle Manager, book experiences, and request housekeeping services instantly.
Are pets allowed in Terra & Tide properties?
Many of our residences are pet-friendly. Please check the specific property details or filter by "Pet Friendly" during your search. A small cleaning fee may apply.
What is your cancellation policy?
We offer a full refund for cancellations made at least 14 days before check-in. For cancellations within 7-14 days, a 50% refund is provided. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable.
